title=[B41] Working Phones 
description=[h1]Working Phones[/h1]
description=This mod adds real, usable phones to the game.
description=
description=Find a phone, open it from your inventory, and use it like a small device inside the game. Call other phones, send messages, save contacts, set alarms, write notes, play games, change ringtones, and customize your smartphone.
description=
description=Every physical phone has its own identity. A phone number belongs to the phone, not to the player. This means one player can carry more than one phone, and each phone can have different contacts, settings, messages, alarms, sounds, and notes.
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=[h2]Phones Included[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Classic 2210 Phone[/b]
description=An old monochrome keypad phone inspired by classic mobile phones. It has a compact launcher, simple pixel-style apps, keypad-style navigation, and classic phone tones.
description=
description=[*][b]Generic Smartphone[/b]
description=A modern phone interface with lock screen, app grid, wallpapers, light/dark themes, icons, touch-style controls, emoji messages, and smartphone sounds.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Main Things You Can Do[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Call other phones[/b] - dial another phone number and make that phone ring.
description=[*][b]Send messages[/b] - write text messages (emojis included) to other phone numbers.
description=[*][b]Use contacts[/b] - save names and numbers, then call or message them faster.
description=[*][b]Use notifications[/b] - incoming calls and messages show on the phone (if the phone is not open a Toast notification will appear on the left side of the screen, added by ElyonLib).
description=[*][b]Use utility apps[/b] - calculator, calendar, clock, alarms, journal, map, settings, and sounds.
description=[*][b]Play games[/b] - Snake, Tetris and on smartphones Chess.
description=[*][b]Customize your phone[/b] - sounds, volume, display name, smartphone theme and wallpapers.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Apps Included[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Phone[/b] - call contacts, answer/decline calls, and view call history.
description=[*][b]Messages[/b] - phone-to-phone conversations with emoji support.
description=[*][b]Contacts[/b] - add, edit, delete, call, and message saved contacts.
description=[*][b]Clock[/b] - multiple alarms, custom alarm names, selected days, and alarm tone choice.
description=[*][b]Calendar[/b] - view dates and navigate months.
description=[*][b]Calculator[/b] - basic calculator app.
description=[*][b]Journal[/b] - create and edit notes on the phone.
description=[*][b]Map[/b] - small phone map view.
description=[*][b]Sounds[/b] - ringtone, notification tone, alarm tone, volume, sound/vibrate/silent mode.
description=[*][b]Settings[/b] - phone display, theme, wallpaper, and other options.
description=[*][b]Games[/b] - game hub and score support.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Sounds, Alarms, And Themes[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]7 classic phone tones[/b] for the Classic 2210.
description=[*][b]3 smartphone ringtones[/b] for calls and message notifications.
description=[*][b]3 smartphone alarm tones[/b] for the Clock app.
description=[*][b]Short vibration[/b] for messages and notifications.
description=[*][b]Long vibration[/b] for calls and alarms.
description=[*][b]Sound / Vibrate / Silent modes[/b].
description=[*][b]Per-phone volume setting[/b].
description=[*][b]Light and dark smartphone themes[/b].
description=[*][b]12 smartphone wallpapers[/b]: 6 color wallpapers and 6 texture wallpapers.
description=[*][b]1,200+ emoji icons[/b] for smartphone messages.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]How To Use[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Find a phone in the world.
description=[*]Right-click the phone in your inventory.
description=[*]Choose [b]Open Phone[/b].
description=[*]The phone creates its own phone number the first time it is used.
description=[*]Add contacts manually or from nearby opened phones.
description=[*]Use Messages to send text to another phone number.
description=[*]Use Phone to call another phone number.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Controls[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Mouse support for buttons, lists, apps, and smartphone UI.
description=[*]Keyboard navigation for phone menus and classic phone controls.
description=[*]Controller-friendly navigation.
description=[*]Key bindings can be changed from the game options.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]World Spawning[/h2]
description=Phones can spawn naturally in the world.
description=
description=Classic phones spawn by default. Smartphones are disabled from spawning by default because they are more powerful and more modern. But can be enabled for spawning in Sandbox Options.
description=
description=Phones can appear in places that make sense, such as electronics shelves, bedrooms, living rooms, offices, desks, lockers, pawn shops and similar containers.
description=
description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Phone container spawns (%)[/b] - more or fewer phones in world containers. 100 is normal, 200 doubles it, 50 halves it, 0 disables container phone spawns.
description=[*][b]Phone zombie spawns (%)[/b] - more or fewer phones found on zombies.
description=[*][b]Spawn classic phones[/b] - enable or disable classic phone spawning.
description=[*][b]Spawn smartphones[/b] - enable or disable smartphone spawning. Disabled by default.
description=[*][b]Nearby phones radius[/b] - distance used when finding nearby opened phones.
description=[*][b]Phone messages per contact[/b] - message history limit per conversation.
description=[*][b]Phone call history limit[/b] - maximum call records saved on each phone.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Multiplayer[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Designed with multiplayer in mind.
description=[*]Messages are phone-to-phone, not player-to-player.
description=[*]A player can carry multiple phones.
description=[*]Incoming calls show who is calling when the number is known.
description=[*]If a phone is busy, the caller will be told that the target is unavailable.
description=[*]Phone sounds can be heard by nearby players.
description=[*]Audible ringing and alarms can attract zombies, depending on volume and sound mode.
description=[/list]
description=

description=[list]
description=[*][b]ElyonLib[/b]

description=[/list]
